52 questions to answer for your leads as a new marketing agency or freelancer

Sounds like a lot, we know. If you do this and make it your own content strategy, your people will thank you!

Basic Business Questions

  • What exactly does your digital marketing service include?
  • How long have you been in business?
  • Do you have predictable revenue or a clear path to it?
  • Are you a local agency or do you work remotely?
  • How many clients do you currently have?
  • Can I see examples of your previous work?
  • Do you have case studies showing actual booking increases?

Pricing & Investment

  • How much do your services cost?
  • What's included in your monthly fee?
  • Are there setup fees or one-time costs?
  • Do you require long-term contracts?
  • What's your minimum budget requirement?
  • How do you bill - monthly, quarterly, annually?
  • Are there any hidden fees I should know about?

Results & Performance

  • How quickly will I see results?
  • What kind of booking increase can I expect?
  • How do you measure success?
  • What if I don't see results - do you offer guarantees?
  • Can you show me real numbers from similar businesses?
  • How do you track and report on performance?
  • What's considered a realistic timeline for growth?

Strategy & Approach

  • What's your process for getting started?
  • How do you determine the right strategy for my business?
  • Do you handle social media, Google ads, or both?
  • Will you create content for my business?
  • How do you handle my brand voice and messaging?
  • Do you do website work or just marketing?
  • How do you research my target audience?

Communication & Management

  • Who will be my main point of contact?
  • How often will we meet or talk?
  • How do you communicate progress and updates?
  • Can I make changes to campaigns once they're running?
  • How quickly do you respond to questions or concerns?
  • What happens if I'm not happy with something?

Technical & Platform Questions

  • Which platforms do you advertise on?
  • Do you manage my Google My Business listing?
  • Will you need access to my current accounts?
  • What tools and software do you use?
  • Do you provide website analytics and reporting?
  • Can you integrate with my booking system?
  • Do you handle review management?

Business Logistics

  • What information do you need from me to get started?
  • How much time will I need to dedicate to this?
  • Do you work with businesses in my specific industry?
  • What if I already have some marketing in place?
  • Can you work with my existing website?
  • Do you provide training so I can eventually do this myself?

Risk & Commitment

  • What happens if I want to cancel?
  • Do I own the ad accounts and content you create?
  • What if my business is seasonal?
  • Can I pause services during slow periods?
  • What's your refund or cancellation policy?
