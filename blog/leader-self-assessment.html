Core Business Health

  • Can you explain what you do and who you serve in one clear sentence?
  • Are you profitable, or do you know exactly when you will be?
  • Do you have predictable revenue or a clear path to it?
  • Are customers finding you without you having to chase them?

Leadership & Operations

  • Do you spend most of your time working on the business or stuck in it?
  • When you're away for a week, does everything fall apart?
  • Can each person on your team clearly state their primary responsibility?
  • Do you make decisions quickly, or do things stall waiting for perfect information?

Growth & Sustainability

  • Are you solving a real problem people will pay to fix?
  • Do customers stick around and buy more, or do you constantly need new ones?
  • Is your biggest constraint demand, capacity, or something else entirely?
  • Are you building systems that work without you, or are you the bottleneck?

Reality Check

  • What would an outside investor say is your biggest weakness right now?
  • If you had to focus on only three things for the next 90 days, what would they be?
  • Are you growing because of your strategy or despite it?
